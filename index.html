Pantry raid

This app allows you to search for a recipe by entering one or more ingredients you have in your fridge or pantry. User can enter the name of ingredient(s) into the search bar and choose the max number of recipes you want to get back and after that click on "find recipe". User will be able to see the list of recipes, choose any of them and then he can check recipe web-site or watch the recipe on Youtube.
